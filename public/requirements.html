Membership Requirements

To remain in good standing each semester, all members must meet the following:

Requirement Minimum Details
Service Hours 30 hours Earned through council events, community service, or school-sponsored activities.
Meeting Attendance 5 meetings You must check in at each meeting to receive credit.

Additionally no representative can have any disciplinary action of any kind against them.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please reach out to a Student Council officer.
